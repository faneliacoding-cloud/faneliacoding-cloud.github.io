Meet DJ Escaflowne

House Of Fanelia is more than a channel — it's a movement. Founded by DJ Escaflowne, it's the home of genre-defying beats that blend House, Electronic, R&B, and Hip-Hop into something you've never heard before.

Every track is crafted with original production and paired with stunning anime-inspired visuals that make each video a cinematic experience. Whether you're looking for a late-night vibe, a workout banger, or something to just zone out to — this is your place.

Born from a love of music and anime culture, House Of Fanelia represents the fusion of sound and art. The beat never stops.
